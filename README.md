## Projects worth looking at

### [🌱 Spore Scout](https://github.com/IshaanG165/Spore-Scout) &nbsp;·&nbsp; 🏆 Hackathon Winner — Canva × USYD
**AI-powered Flutter mobile app for real-time crop disease detection.**
Built with a team of 5 at the Canva × USYD hackathon. The app uses a TFLite on-device AI model to analyse photos of crop leaves and detect 8 conditions — fungal stress, nitrogen deficiency, bacterial spot, viral infection, and more. Pairs with ESP32 Bluetooth environmental sensors for real-time humidity and temperature. Generates eco-friendly organic treatment recommendations. Fully offline-capable.

My contributions: AI model integration (TFLiteService), Bluetooth sensor data pipeline (SensorService), scan history with Hive local persistence, and the results UI.

> Built in hours. Shipped. Won. Flutter + on-device AI + hardware sensors in one app — the most technically ambitious thing I've built under pressure.

`Flutter` `Dart` `TFLite` `Bluetooth / ESP32` `Hive` `Android` `iOS`

### [🗺️ RouteScout — Transport Intelligence Dashboard](https://github.com/IshaanG165/RouteScout) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live →](https://route-scout-rho.vercel.app)
**Full-stack NSW bus route analysis, simulation, and optimisation platform.**
Interactive dark-map dashboard (React Leaflet + CartoDB), FastAPI backend with Pydantic v2, simulation engine (add/remove stops, adjust frequency, see real-time metric deltas), AI-generated route recommendations, and scenario comparison charts. Data schema mirrors the real NSW Open Transport API.

> The kind of project that could actually be a product. Built it to look like one.

`Next.js` `FastAPI` `Python` `TypeScript` `React Leaflet` `Zustand` `Recharts`

### [📱 QuickFix QR — Real-Time Issue Reporting](https://github.com/IshaanG165/QuickFix-QR)
**QR-powered infrastructure reporting with a live admin dashboard.**
